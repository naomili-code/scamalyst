The Ethics of AI Detection

Question: Is it ethical to detect AI-generated text?

Our position: Yes‚Äîwhen the goal is protection, not surveillance. ScamSensei uses AI detection to help users recognize manipulation patterns, not to shame writing styles or restrict legitimate AI use.

Core principles:

  • Transparency over secrecy
  • User safety over alarmism
  • Education over blame
  • User benefit over data extraction

Limitations

  • Works best on English text (multi-language support is a future goal)
  • Heuristic analysis can produce false positives or false negatives
  • Does not verify sender identity via technical mail checks like DMARC/SPF
  • Does not guarantee that a link is malicious‚Äîonly that it appears suspicious
  • Scam tactics evolve continuously, so detection requires ongoing updates
