Ethics & Transparency

Our Ethical Goal

ScamSensei exists to protect users through clarity, not fear.

We focus on helping people understand manipulation patterns so they can make safer decisions on their own.

What AI Is Used For

AI supports pattern recognition for urgency language, suspicious requests, and risky link behavior.

It also helps surface plain-language explanations so users learn why something may be unsafe.

What We Do Not Claim

ScamSensei does not claim perfect accuracy and does not replace human judgment.

It is an educational safety tool, not legal advice and not a guarantee.

Privacy Promise

Your analysis stays with you: no saved messages, no personal-data collection, and no tracking cookies.

Analysis runs locally in your browser, so we cannot see what you submit.

Core Principles

Transparency

Explain decisions clearly instead of hiding logic.

User Safety

Reduce harm and avoid alarmist design choices.

Education

Teach patterns so users build long-term judgment.

User Benefit

Prioritize people over data extraction.

Current Limits

ScamSensei works best with English text and heuristic detection can produce false positives or false negatives.

It does not verify sender identity with mail-level checks (like DMARC/SPF) and cannot guarantee whether a link is truly malicious.

Disclaimer

ScamSensei provides guidance, not guarantees. Always verify suspicious messages independently, especially when money, credentials, or personal information are involved.
